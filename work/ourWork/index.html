Case Studies

Technology projects to save money and increase efficiency

Advising a large public company through re-designing their software licensing and usage.

Combining management consulting and business analysis, we helped a large public company (more than 2,000 employees) completely re-design their software licensing and usage. In doing so, the company expanded their response to changes in the business environment, while reducing their recurring licensing costs by 40% and sustainment costs by 75%.

Designing a new application with clear business objectives in mind

Streamlining a transportation company’s logistics process through building a custom application.

We built a custom application designed to streamline the management and planning of a transportation company’s entire logistics process. The company executives now refer to the new operational dashboard as a “single source of truth”. They use the app to manage the movement of product through the logistics chain; from the warehouse to independent retail stores across North America, and they credit the new technology with helping them transform and scale to become one of the fastest growing companies in their sector.

Assessing a software portfolio to take on strategic initiatives

Road-mapping an application portfolio to grow a micro-brewery from a start-up into a major player in the hospitality industry.

We developed an application road-map for a start-up micro-brewery based on their future business plans. This allowed them to replace spreadsheets with a fit-for-purpose ERP to manage logistics and meet their financial requirements. They now focus their attention on strategy and the future, while allowing the integrated solution address day to day tasks.

Leveraging data analytics to increase academic achievement

Leveraging data analytics for a large regional school system to better understand student pathways and the impact on their eventual academic achievement.

We created a framework of intelligent data agents for a regional school system to quickly aggregate and manage their disparate data formats and sources into a single knowledge graph. Our data scientists partnered with the school system’s team to examine the graph and test and explore various hypotheses about student outcomes. This data allowed the school administration to make adjustments to student education options, scheduling and curriculum. The result: raising overall level of academic attainment and achievement.
