Driven by Community

Our commitment to our community sets us apart. People are at our core and we strive to build strong connections with everyone we encounter. These connections drive our work, as well as our deep involvement in the community.

Our People

We’re more than just our collective skillset and what we do when we’re “at the office”. The Arcurve culture starts with an interest in all things technical, and thrives because of a commitment to teamwork, mutual trust and respect and continuous learning. We support one another in all that we do; work, life, play and family.

Technology Growth

Technology is everywhere. We know the possibilities in our space are endless and we see a great need to encourage others to pursue professions in new technologies and software. We actively seek opportunities to teach, mentor, support and contribute to our industry. Our team of professionals share their expertise (and opinions!) year-round at post-secondary institutions, through industry partnerships and community programs year-round, not to mention, our Arcurve student intern program is one of the best in Canada.

Giving Back Locally

We believe in the importance of giving back to the communities we work and live in. We lead as a founding partner of YYC TechGives, providing a platform for other tech organizations to join us and participate in community giving. Additionally, we actively support the Calgary Food Bank, are Partners for Life with the Canadian Blood Services, a supporter of Beakerhead and we also support many of our employees’ favourite causes. We give to what matters to our people.
